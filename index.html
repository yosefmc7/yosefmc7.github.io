My Messenger Dog

So, this dog shows up at our door about 9:30 on a cold January night in 2015. We told her to go home, but she obviously wanted in out of the cold, and she looked hungry. So, I decided to shut the door and check if she was still there after a while. She was....standing there looking at me like "can I come in now?" It was getting late, and she was cold and hungry. So, we let her in for the night. She spent the night and now she's been with us for over three years!
